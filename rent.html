New York Jukebox delivers fully refurbished 1954 Seeburg HF100R jukeboxes with fifty records and printed title strips installed. The jukebox will be tested at the event site to insure correct operation, and collected within 24 hours after the event. At time of delivery, we will demonstrate the use of the jukebox, and leave written instructions as well. Unless you request otherwise, the jukebox will be set on “free play,” and will not require coins. In the unlikely event of a problem with the jukebox during your event, we’ll send someone out immediately to restore it to working order.

Though this jukebox is associated with the 1950s, any kind of music sounds great on it. Many contemporary artists insist that their music be released on vinyl, and a surprising number of small labels continue to issue recordings both old and new. We can provide familiar hits or offbeat obscurities, and you can participate in the record selection as much or as little as you like. Records are selected on the basis of your answers to the questionnaire on the “Select Records” page. You are welcome to take the survey without committing to renting a jukebox.

To rent a jukebox, you will need to provide a suitable location- dry, level, & secure, with access to electricity—and ensure that we can access it at time of delivery and collection. A jukebox can be delivered over a few steps, but cannot be taken up or down a flight of stairs. You will need to provide contacts for payment, delivery and collection. There must be someone at the event who will call if there are any operational questions.

Our formal rental agreement assigns you responsibility for any damage to or loss of the jukebox while in your location, including by theft, fire, water or any other cause. Our liability is limited to the charge for rental. Refunds and/or discounts are available only if there is a disabling malfunction during the event.

To request a rental, click the Rental Form button below, and complete the form. A formal agreement will be sent by email for your review. We require a $100 deposit to reserve a jukebox, with the balance of $375 plus sales tax paid at time of delivery.
