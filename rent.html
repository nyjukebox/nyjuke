A classic 1950s-era Seeburg jukebox is the perfect accompaniment to events of all kinds – parties, celebrations, business meetings, any occasion enhanced by music of your choice.

We will set up a fully refurbished 1954 Seeburg HF100R jukebox. Fifty 45 rpm records of your choice will be installed, with printed title strips. The jukebox will be tested at the event site to insure correct operation, and collected within 24 hours after the event. At time of delivery, we will demonstrate the use of the jukebox, and leave written instructions as well. Unless you request otherwise, the jukebox will be set on “free play,” and will not require coins. In the unlikely event of a problem with the jukebox during your event, we’ll send someone out immediately to restore it to working order.

Though this jukebox is associated with the 1950s, any kind of music sounds great on it. We want you to be excited about the records in the jukebox, whether you prefer familiar hits or offbeat obscurities. Many artists continue to insist that their music be released on vinyl, and a surprisingly number of small labels issue old and new recordings. You can choose any level of engagement in selecting the records, or you can hand that off to us, instead choosing from general themes. The particulars of the record selection process are on the “Select Records” page.

You will need to provide a suitable location- dry, level, & secure, with access to electricity—and ensure that we can access it at time of delivery and collection. The jukebox can be delivered over a few steps, but cannot be taken up or down stairs. You will need to provide contacts for payment, delivery and collection. There must be someone at the event who will call if there are any operational questions.

Our formal rental agreement assigns you responsibility for any damage to or loss of the jukebox while in your location, including by theft, fire, water or any other cause. Our liability is limited to the charge for rental. Refunds and/or discounts are available only if there is a disabling malfunction during the event.

After you fill in the form below, a formal agreement will be sent by email for your approval.
